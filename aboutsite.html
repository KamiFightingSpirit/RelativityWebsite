About This Site:

Hello and thanks for taking the time to visit my site! To get right to it, the big idea is that you are an astronaut (or Space Cowboy for fellow Cowboy Bebop fans) exploring the universe and experiencing the fascinating effects of time dilation; a favorite subject of mine. The site itself is a digital resume and, by clicking the planets in the solar system it walks you through my professional experience. As each of the planets covers a period of my life and the amount of time you take to go through the site covers a period in yours, I thought it would be fun to explore that relative time cost via time dilation.

How it Works:

By clicking a planet to read a portion of the site the "External Time" portion of the time dilation banner at the top of each page will increase. Additionally, for every second you remain on the site a day passes on your hypothetical intergalactic ship.

Why:

Most importantly, this was my first major project as I continue to learn web development and improve my programming skills. As such, it is an educational tool and also a piece of my portfolio for others to see.

As an educational tool it has been incredible. I didn't set out thinking I would do something within the animation realm but it took that path as my goal was ambitious. This meant learning the frameworks for Web GL as well as the Pixi.js library that I used for the graphical elements on the site.
